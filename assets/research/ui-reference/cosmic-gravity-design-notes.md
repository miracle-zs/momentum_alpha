# Cosmic Gravity Design Notes

Reference image:

- `cosmic-gravity-ui-reference.jpeg`

## Visual Direction

- Overall tone: near-black space background with restrained gold highlights.
- Mood: premium, quiet, technical, and slightly futuristic.
- Contrast: low-key surfaces with selective glow on primary actions and focal data.

## Layout Principles

- Use a left-heavy identity block with supporting content arranged in a clear grid.
- Separate content into distinct visual zones rather than one continuous dashboard band.
- Keep cards narrow enough to feel like instrument panels, not generic app tiles.
- Maintain generous spacing between major regions, but keep intra-card spacing compact.

## Color Language

- Base background: very dark black and deep space tones.
- Surface layers: subtle gradients, not flat fills.
- Accent color: warm stardust gold.
- Secondary accent: muted cool blue/teal only for balance and contrast.
- Typography: soft white with muted gray secondary text.

## Component Language

- Buttons: pill shapes with thin borders and soft glow for the primary action.
- Tags: outlined capsules with color-coded emphasis.
- Cards: thin borders, dark surfaces, subtle inner depth, limited shadow.
- Indicators: small dots, rings, and bars that feel like dashboard instrumentation.
- Typography: large condensed title, smaller geometric labels, restrained body copy.

## What To Reuse In The Dashboard

- Dark gold-on-black palette.
- Section hierarchy with clear labels and compact status blocks.
- Dense but ordered data cards.
- Glowing primary action treatment.
- Minimal decorative noise with only a few intentional visual highlights.

## What To Avoid

- Flat white backgrounds.
- Generic admin dashboard blues.
- Overly soft, bubbly UI treatment.
- Large empty cards without a clear informational role.
- Excessive glow or neon effects that overpower the data.
